Spirited Away

Mavis receives an e-mail with a picture of the newborn daughter of her high school boyfriend Buddy Slade. Believing this to be a sign she and Buddy are meant to be together, Mavis leaves Minneapolis and returns to her hometown of Mercury, Minnesota, to reclaim her life with Buddy.
